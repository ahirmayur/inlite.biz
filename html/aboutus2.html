Company's History

Established in the year 1948, 'Universal Trading Co.' is a remarkable trader and supplier of LED Light Fittings as well as other Indoor - Outdoor Light Fittings and Accessories. Owing to our immaculate track record of more than six decades, we have emerged as a dependable name in the realm. We procure authentic products that meet the requirements of industry standards. Easy to install, excellent back up, dimensionally accurate and super low wattage operation are some of the distinct attributes of our products that make them set apart from the rest. Our products are in wide demand across the households, commercial establishments and other sectors.

Our tie-ups with some of the reputed vendors of our domain has has befitted us in carrying out all business operations with ease and accuracy. No procured product passes unchecked and hence after the approval of our quality inspectors, we package these products. These products enjoy the market credibility and hence are in wide demand across the whole market. The products supplied by us are competitively priced.

Our company is headed by 'Mr. Naresh L. Shahri'. Under his mentorship, our company has been able to retain its long list of clientele without much difficulty. His focused approach coupled with his farsightedness has backed us in offering our customers apt solutions.
